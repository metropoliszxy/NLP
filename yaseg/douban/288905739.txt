请教——檀特山

文章里一段翻译，涉及檀特山，如下：Dantaloka was a mountain near Varucha, with a cavern(now called Kashmiri-Ghar) where Sudana lived (or, as another tradition has it, the place where the young Siddhartha, the future Buddha Sakyamuni, underwent his ascetic sufferings before achieving his enlightenment).试译：檀特山位于乌苌国（Varucha）附近，有须大拏所居山洞（今称Kashmiri-Ghar）。另一传说则云此地悉达多，未来的释迦牟尼佛年少时成道以前苦行之所。经查，这段文字出于Soothill的A Dictionary of Chinese Buddhist Terms.前者传说应出《太子须大拏经》，後者还没有仔细查。问题：对中亚史地不熟，不知道专名有无错误。Varucha，Kashmiri-Ghar中译是什么？
