Australian Government response to mistreatment of Australian cattle in Indonesian abattoirs

Australian Government action on live exportsOn 7 June 2011 the Minister for Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry, the Hon. Senator Joe Ludwig, suspended live animal exports to Indonesia for slaughter following evidence of animal welfare abuses in some abattoirs.The Australian Government condemns the mistreatment of animals and is working to introduce new measures to ensure appropriate animal welfare standards are met across all live export markets right through to point of slaughter.To find out more about Australian Government action on live exports, including media releases and communiqués, visit 我操，这tm是真的，不是在搞笑。
