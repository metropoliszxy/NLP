Psychic rogues are proficient with light armor, but not with shields. 每日灵能点数：灵能游荡者的显能能力由其可用的灵能点数所限制。他的每日灵能点数由灵能游荡者职业列表列出。另外，如果他具有高智力值，还可以获得每日额外灵能点数（参见属性调整值与额外灵能点数）。他的种族也可能提供每日额外灵能点数，某些专长与物品亦然。一级灵能游荡者不会从职业属性获得每日灵能点数，但他仍可获得额外灵能点数（如果他智力够班）并可用这些灵能点显现他已知的心灵异能。Power Points/Day: A psychic rogue's ability to manifest powers is limited by the power points he has available. His base daily allotment of power points is given on Table: The Psychic Rogue. In addition, he receives bonus power points per day if he has a high Intelligence score (see Table: Ability Modifiers and Bonus Power Points). His race may also provide bonus power points per day, as may certain feats and items. A 1st-level psychic rogue gains no power points for his class level, but he gains bonus power points (if he is entitled to any) and can manifest the single power he knows with those power points.已知心灵异能：游戏开始时你可以选择一个灵能游荡者的心灵异能做已知异能。除了第4级，第8级，第12级，第16级和第20级，在他得到每个新等级时，他都会获得一个新灵能的知识。Powers Known: A psychic rogue begins play knowing one psychic rogue power of your choice. With the exception of 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 20th levels, each time he achieves a new level, he unlocks the knowledge of a new power.新异能可以从灵能游荡者的异能列表中选取。(例外：灵能专长“扩展知识”和传奇专长“传奇扩展知识”，允许灵能游荡者学习列表以及其他职业的心灵异能。)灵能游荡者可以展现灵能点花费等于或低于其显能者等级的任何异能。Choose the powers known from the psychic rogue power list. (Exception: The feats Expanded Knowledge and Epic Expanded Knowledge do allow a psychic rogue to learn powers from the lists of other classes.) A psychic rogue can manifest any power that has a power point cost equal to or lower than his manifester level.灵能游荡者每日展现的灵能总数只为他的每日灵能点所限制。The total number of powers a psychic rogue can manifest in a day is limited only by his daily power points.灵能游荡者仅需要知道他的异能，它们根植于他的意识之中。他不需要事先准备它们(像某些施法者准备法术那样)，虽然他仍然需要一整夜的睡眠才能恢复使用过的灵能点数。A psychic rogue simply knows his powers; they are ingrained in his mind. He does not need to prepare them (in the way that some spellcasters prepare their spells), though he must get a good night's sleep each day to regain all his spent power points.豁免灵能游荡者异能的难度等级为10 + 异能能级 + 灵能游荡者的智力调整值。The Difficulty Class for saving throws against psychic rogue powers is 10 + the power's level + the psychic rogue's Intelligence modifier.最高可知能级：游戏开始时灵能游荡者具有学习1级异能的能力。当他获得更高等级，他可能会获得掌握更复杂异能的能力。Maximum Power Level Known: A psychic rogue begins play with the ability to learn 1st-level powers. As he attains higher levels, he may gain the ability to master more complex powers.要发掘或展现一道异能，灵能游荡者至少需要有10 + 该异能能级的智力值。To learn or manifest a power, a psychic rogue must have an Intelligence score of at least 10 + the power's level. 偷袭规则：……其实也是废话一箩筐，规则基本同游荡者，值得注意的区别是两个游荡者等级加1d6，灵能游荡者则是三级加1d6，具体见职业列表，描述如下： 偷袭：如果灵能游荡者能在目标无法有效防卫自己的攻击时抓住时机，他就可以对目标的要害攻击并造成额外的伤害。Sneak Attack: If a psychic rogue can catch an opponent when she cannot defend herself effectively from his attack, he can strike a vital spot for extra damage.只要对方失去了AC上的敏捷加值（无论目标是否有敏捷加值），或者当灵能游荡者夹击他的目标时，他的攻击就可以造成额外伤害。在一级时此额外伤害为1d6，之后每三个灵能游荡者等级可以再增加1d6的伤害。如果灵能游荡者在偷袭中造成重击，额外伤害不能翻倍。Basically, the psychic rogue's attack deals extra damage any time his target would be denied a Dexterity bonus to AC (whether the target actually has a Dexterity bonus or not), or when the psychic rogue flanks his target. This extra damage is 1d6 at 1st level, and it increases by 1d6 every three psychic rogue levels thereafter. Should the psychic rogue score a critical hit with a sneak attack, this extra damage is not multiplied.远程攻击也可以造成偷袭，但目标必须在30尺内。Ranged attacks can count as sneak attacks only if the target is within 30 feet.使用闷棍或徒手攻击时，灵能游荡者可以在偷袭时造成非致命伤害而不是一般伤害。他不能在偷袭中用造成一般伤害的武器造成非致命伤害，即使使用通常的-4减值方式也不可以。With a sap (blackjack) or an unarmed strike, a psychic rogue can make a sneak attack that deals nonlethal damage instead of lethal damage. He cannot use a weapon that deals lethal damage to deal nonlethal damage in a sneak attack, not even with the usual -4 penalty, because he must make optimal use of his weapon to execute a sneak attack.灵能游荡者只能偷袭那些有明显身体结构的活物，不死生物、构装体、泥形怪物、植物类生物，以及虚体生物都没有可供偷袭的要害部位。任何免疫重击的生物也不会受到偷袭伤害。游荡者必须能够仔细观察目标找到要害部位，而且必须能接触到该部位。灵能游荡者无法偷袭那些有隐蔽的生物，也无法通过攻击那些无法接触要害生物的突出部位来造成偷袭。A psychic rogue can sneak attack only living creatures with discernible anatomies -- undead, constructs, oozes, plants, and incorporeal creatures lack vital areas to attack. Any creature that is immune to critical hits is not vulnerable to sneak attacks. The psychic rogue must be able to see the target well enough to pick out a vital spot and must be able to reach such a spot. A psychic rogue cannot sneak attack while striking a creature with concealment or striking the limbs of a creature whose vitals are beyond reach.寻找陷阱基本同游荡者，区别在于增加灵能陷阱的说明。 寻找陷阱：灵能游荡者可以用搜索技能来寻找那些难度等级在20以上的陷阱。Trapfinding: Psychic rogues can use the Search skill to locate traps when the task has a Difficulty Class higher than 20.找到非魔法陷阱的DC至少为20，如果隐藏的比较好还会更高。找到魔法或灵能陷阱的DC等于25+制造此陷阱法术的法术等级。Finding a nonmagical trap has a DC of at least 20, or higher if it is well hidden. Finding a magic or psionic trap has a DC of 25 + the level of the spell or power used to create it.灵能游荡者可以用解除装置技能来拆卸魔法或者灵能陷阱。拆卸魔法陷阱的DC等于25+制造此陷阱法术的法术等级。Psychic rogues can use the Disable Device skill to disarm magic and psionic traps. Magic and psionic traps generally have a DC of 25 + the level of the spell or power used to create them.如果解除装置技能检定结果高过陷阱DC10或以上，灵能游荡者可以研究此陷阱，看出它如何运作，还可以不拆卸而通过陷阱（连同队友）。A psychic rogue who beats a trap's DC by 10 or more with a Disable Device check can study a trap, figure out how it works, and bypass it (with his party) without disarming it. 反射闪避：从第2级开始，灵能游荡者能以超凡的灵活闪避魔法和不同寻常的攻击。有些攻击效果若通过反射检定可以减少一半伤害，如果灵能游荡者遭到此类攻击，在通过反射检定后他将不受伤害。灵能游荡者只有在穿轻甲或不穿甲时才可以进行反射闪避。无助状态下的灵能游荡者不能进行反射闪避。Evasion (Ex): At 2nd level and higher, a psychic rogue can avoid even magical and unusual attacks with great agility. If he makes a successful Reflex saving throw against an attack that normally deals half damage on a successful save, he instead takes no damage. Evasion can be used only if the psychic rogue is wearing light armor or no armor. A helpless psychic rogue does not gain the benefit of evasion. 危险感知（超自然能力）：在第5级，灵能游荡者将得到感知危险的能力。这种能力使灵能游荡者等同于受到“危险感知”心灵异能的影响。这种能力能够持久发挥作用，只要灵能游荡者保持灵能聚焦。Danger Sense (Su): At 5th level, a psychic rogue gains the ability to augment his ability to sense danger. When active, this ability grants the psychic rogue the effects of the danger sense power. This ability is active as long as the psychic rogue maintains psionic focus.在第7级，灵能游荡者的危险感知能力获得了增幅效果，他得到了直接闪避能力。当他的危险感知能力发挥作用的时候，他在措手不及时或被不可见的攻击者攻击时依然保留AC上的敏捷加值。不过如果他不能动，他还是会丢掉AC上的敏捷加值。如果灵能游荡者已经由其他职业（譬如拥有2个野蛮人等级的灵能游荡者）获得了直觉闪避能力，那么他自动获得精通直觉闪避（见下文）。At 7th level, the psychic rogue's danger sense ability gains the effects of a single augmentation and he is granted the effects of the uncanny dodge ability. While his danger sense ability is active, the psychic rogue can react to danger before his senses would normally allow him to do so. He retains his Dexterity bonus to AC (if any) even if he is caught flat-footed or struck by an invisible attacker. However, he still loses his Dexterity bonus to AC if immobilized. If a psychic rogue already has uncanny dodge from a different class (a psychic rogue with at least two levels of barbarian, for example), he automatically gains improved uncanny dodge (see below) instead.在第9级，他得到了第二个增幅效果，得到了精通直觉闪避能力。当他的危险感知能力发挥作用的时候，灵能游荡者不再受到夹击。其他游荡者无法以夹击方式偷袭此角色，除非攻击者的游荡者等级比目标的游荡者等级高至少4级。如果角色已经因为其他职业获得直觉闪避能力（见上文），该角色在得到直觉闪避时自动获得精通直觉闪避，并且将所有获得直觉闪避职业的等级累加起来以决定夹击此角色所需的最低游荡者等级。At 9th level, he gains a second augmentation and is granted the effects of the improved uncanny dodge ability. While his danger sense ability is active, the psychic rogue cannot be flanked; he can react to opponents on opposite sides of him as easily as he can react to a single attacker. This defense denies another rogue the ability to sneak attack the character by flanking him, unless the attacker has at least four more rogue levels than the target does. If a character already has uncanny dodge (see above) from a second class, the character automatically gains improved uncanny dodge instead, and the levels from the classes that grant uncanny dodge stack to determine the minimum rogue level required to flank the character.特殊能力：11级时，以及之后每三级（14，17，20级），游荡者可以在以下特殊能力中选择一种。Special Abilities: On attaining 11th level, and at every three levels thereafter (14th, 17th, and 20th), a psychic rogue gains a special ability of his choice from among the following options. 盲点（类灵能）：灵能游荡者可以每天一次使用这项能力。灵能游荡者可以让任何单一生物完全感觉不到他的存在，一如显现“遮蔽心灵”灵能，显能等级为他的灵能游荡者等级。如果目标生物未能通过豁免，灵能游荡者可以一直隐藏，只要他保持专注于这项能力。Blind Spot (Ps): The psychic rogue can use this ability once per day. While in effect, the psychic rogue can attempt to make himself completely undetectable to any single creature as if manifesting the cloud mind power with a manifester level equal to his psychic rogue level. If the target creature fails its save, the psychic rogue can remain hidden as long as he maintains concentration on this ability.替身（类灵能）：每天一次，灵能游荡者可以制造一个复制自己的幻象。这个幻觉影像在计算施法者等级时等于他的灵能游荡者等级。持续时间：专注，最长1轮/施法者等级。Decoy (Ps): Once a day, the psychic rogue can create an illusionary duplicate of himself. The illusion functions as a project image spell with a caster level equal to his psychic rogue level, except that it lasts as long as he maintains concentration, to a maximum of 1 round per caster level.增强偷袭（超自然）：当灵能游荡者已经灵能聚焦，他可以把灵能伤害加入他的偷袭伤害。这项能力强化他的偷袭伤害 +1d6。（譬如，一个11级灵能游荡者可以造成 +5d6的偷袭伤害而不是 +4d6）。这项能力可以被选择三次。Enhanced Sneak Attack (Su): When the psychic rogue has psionic focus, he adds psionic damage to his sneak attacks. This ability may be selected up to three times.精通反射闪避（特异）：这项能力就像反射闪避。有些攻击效果若通过反射检定可以减少一半伤害，如果灵能游荡者遭到此类攻击，在通过反射检定后他将不受伤害，如果检定失败他也只受到一半伤害。Improved Evasion (Ex): This ability works like evasion, except that while the psychic rogue still takes no damage on a successful Reflex saving throw against attacks, he henceforth takes only half damage on a failed save. A helpless psychic rogue does not gain the benefit of improved evasion.意志削弱（超自然）：灵能游荡者学会在他的偷袭中集中心灵能量，干扰敌人的精神。他一次偷袭可造成敌人2点智力属性伤害。受此攻击失去的属性点能够以每天1点的速度恢复。Mind Cripple (Su): A psychic rogue with this ability has learned to focus psychic energy into his sneak attacks, which disrupts the mental capability of his foe. Upon gaining this ability, he selects a number of skills equal to 3 + his Intelligence modifier. When making a skill check with one of these skills, he may take 10 even if stress and distractions would normally prevent him from doing so.
