为独立艺术小册 "呼｜吸"｜征集"大雾" 行为摄影 / The Big Mist: Call for Participants all the world

当呼吸都成了一个大问题时，我们不得不深刻反思，让我们为糟糕的城市环境做点什么吧！一次集体不露脸不出声的呐喊和窒息时刻！在全球各地征集有兴趣及有趣的、具讽刺精神的参与者！一个行为摄影活动，戴上您或您们认为绝对可以防御毒雾的道具、头套、头巾在户外（最好是在糟糕的气候中）用您或您们各自的个性姿势拍照（最好是全身照），后发至ogaoling@gmail.com 乃至微薄 facebook, blog...也计划在不久的将来印成一本独立小杂志...期待您的参与！截止到：毒雾停止日--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------When breathing has become a big problem, we have to reflect deeply, let's do something for the poor urban environment! Make a collective Anonymous & silent cry! Assembling interesting and ironic spirited participants from around the world!  A performance photographic documentation, where participants  put on anti-smog props, caps, scarves outdoors (preferably in bad weather) and anonymously bring out your own personality (preferably showing the whole body). Please email your work to ogaoling@gmail.com or weibo, facebook, blog ...Sowing the seeds of an independent magazine —— " Breathe " Look forward to your participation!
