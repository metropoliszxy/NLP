译诗：复活节周（By Joyce Kilmer）

(纪念约瑟夫·玛丽·普兰克特)(“浪漫的爱尔兰死去并且消失，它随欧利瑞进了坟墓。“)——威廉· 巴特勒·叶芝“浪漫的爱尔兰死去并且消失，它随欧利瑞进了坟墓。”那么，叶芝，是什么给了复活节的黄昏如此清朗华丽的色彩？那天是一场血雨，红色的雨，下在亮丽蓝色的四月天。它赐福给大地，直到它孕育了英勇厚得像石南花在开放。浪漫的爱尔兰永不会死去！欧利瑞躺在肥沃的土地里，歌曲和长矛历经悠悠岁月，在爱国者的坟墓上升起。不朽的爱国者才刚死去，然而他们在过去的岁月里已经流血。在你的眼前升起的是什么旗帜？那振奋你耳朵的又是什么曲调？那年轻的共和国的旗帜在飘扬，向着许多英里外集合的军队。欧康纳的大街吵闹又亲切，队伍一列列像是穿着绿色的菌株。爱尔兰的土壤在悸动，发光，知道此刻就在这里的人们，再次像爱尔兰人一样为自己所珍视的作斗争！爱德华离开了他曾经休息的地方，萨斯菲尔德表情欢喜、斗志昂扬，看那蚂蚁从不安的睡眠里跃起，想要抓住帕特里克·皮尔斯的手。没有绳子能扼杀歌谣，他付出代价不是为了更长的死亡。没有监狱栅栏能使星星暗淡，生石灰侵蚀不了活着的灵魂。浪漫的爱尔兰并没有老去，透露她青春的岁月会闪耀。她的心脏以神圣的面包为食，烈士的血是她的醇酒。附原文：(In memory of Joseph Mary Plunkett)("Romantic Ireland's dead and gone,It's with O'Leary in the grave.")—William Butler Yeats."Romantic Ireland's dead and gone,    It's with O'Leary in the grave."Then, Yeats, what gave that Easter dawn    A hue so radiantly brave?There was a rain of blood that day,    Red rain in gay blue April weather.It blessed the earth till it gave birth    To valour thick as blooms of heather.Romantic Ireland never dies!    O'Leary lies in fertile ground,And songs and spears throughout the years    Rise up where patriot graves are found.Immortal patriots newly dead    And ye that bled in bygone years,What banners rise before your eyes?    What is the tune that greets your ears?The young Republic's banners smile    For many a mile where troops convene.O'Connell street is loudly sweet    With strains of Wearing of the Green.The soil of Ireland throbs and glows    With life that knows the hour is hereTo strike again like Irishmen    For that which Irishmen hold dear.Lord Edward leaves his resting place    And Sarsfield's face is glad and fierce.See Emmet leap from troubled sleep    To grasp the hand of Padraic Pearse!There is no rope can strangle song    And not for long death takes his toll.No prison bars can dim the stars    Nor quicklime eat the living soul.Romantic Ireland is not old.    For years untold her youth shall shine.Her heart is fed on Heavenly bread,    The blood of martyrs is her wine.
