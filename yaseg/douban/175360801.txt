狂喜的笛音

 狂喜的笛音     --KabirI know the sound of the ecstatic flute, but I don’t know whose flute it is.我知道狂喜的笛音，但我不知道那是谁的笛子。A lamp burns and has neither wick nor oil.一盏灯烧了起来却没有灯芯也没有灯油。A lily pad blossoms and is not attached to the bottom!漂浮的莲叶花盛开却没有连接到水底！When one flower opens, ordinarily dozens open.当一朵花开了，通常好几打的花跟着开。The moon bird’s head is filled with nothing but thoughts of the moon, and when the next rain will come is all that the rain bird thinks of.月鸟的脑袋里除了想月亮之外什么也没有，而水鸟所想的只是下次的雨什么时候来。Who is it we spend our entire life loving?我们一生所爱的究竟是谁？
