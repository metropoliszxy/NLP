一个很有意思的说法

前几天跟同学聊天，他提到说北京的那个巨蛋很可能是柯勒律治的忽必烈汗获得的启发，然后找来诗一看，可能确实如此：In Xanadu did Kubla KhanA stately pleasure-dome decree :Where Alph, the sacred river, ranThrough caverns measureless to manDown to a sunless sea. 还有后面的更明显了，一个在水中荡漾的穹顶： The shadow of the dome of pleasure   Floated midway on the waves;   Where was heard the mingled measure   From the fountain and the caves.更别说这个故事的发生地就是大都。
