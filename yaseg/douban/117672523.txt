Obama discusses Korea tensions with Chinese leader

Chinese state media say President Hu Jintao discussed tensions on the Korean peninsula in a phone call with President Barack Obama.The Xinhua News Agency says Hu spoke to Obama on Monday from Beijing and called for all sides to exercise restraint to prevent the situation from deteriorating further.He sais China is deeply concerned over the tensions and regrets the loss of lives and property in last month's artillery exchange between North and South Korea.China is North Korea's most important ally and has been pressured by the U.S. and others to use its influence to rein in Pyongyang following tis shelling of a South Korean island.
